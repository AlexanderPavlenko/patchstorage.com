Machinedrum Part Randomizer

machinedrumrandomizer

Randomly pick a machine and machine parameters per page for the Elektron Machinedrum. Great for finding new ideas in this deep, deep machine.

Press SHIFT to switch between “pick target track” and “pick which parameters to randomize”. Hit “Go” to upload MIDI to the Machinedrum.

Tested on Machinedrum UW+ mk2.
